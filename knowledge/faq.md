# FAQ

## What is unified-support?
unified-support is an AI-native omnichannel customer support system.
Customers can reach you through any messaging app they already use —
WeChat, Telegram, WhatsApp, LINE, Discord, Slack, and more.

## How does it work?
1. Customer scans a QR code or clicks a link
2. They choose their preferred messaging app
3. They start chatting — AI answers instantly using your knowledge base
4. If needed, a human agent takes over seamlessly

## What channels are supported?
We support 19+ messaging channels including:
- WeChat / 企业微信
- Telegram
- WhatsApp
- LINE
- Discord
- Slack
- iMessage
- Matrix
- MS Teams
- And many more

## How do I add my own FAQ?
Add markdown files to the `knowledge/` directory. They are automatically
indexed on startup and used by the AI to answer customer questions.

## How much does it cost?
unified-support is open source and free. You only pay for the AI model
API calls (DeepSeek, Claude, GPT, etc.) and your own hosting.
